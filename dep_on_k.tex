

We investigated the effect of the multi-focus attention beam size $K$
by evaluating performance on the CoNLL test-a dataset for different
values of $K$. Results are shown in \figref{fig:k_effect}.
Performance peaks for $K \in [3,5]$, with a sharp decrease after
$K=10$.  This validates our central premise: all-pairs label coupling
may hurt accuracy.
